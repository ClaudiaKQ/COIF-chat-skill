# COIF Learning Engine

This file stores strategic lessons discovered while using COIF.

---

## Lesson 001

### Initial Assumption

The best opportunity is usually the best job.

### Discovery

Some opportunities create more value through consulting, partnerships, client access, or venture creation than through employment.

### New Rule

Always separate:

1. Employment ranking
2. Business development ranking
3. Combined COIF ranking

### Impact

High

## Lesson 002

### Initial Assumption

A full-time role should rank higher than a part-time role.

### Discovery

Claudia's primary objective is building CBY Impact.

### New Rule

Part-time, fractional, consulting, and partnership opportunities receive preference when they support long-term business growth.

### Impact

High

---

## Lesson 003

### Initial Assumption

Company analysis should focus on the posted role.

### Discovery

The hidden opportunity often exists outside the role.

### New Rule

Always analyze:

* company
* clients
* ecosystem
* partnership potential
* AI opportunities

before final scoring.

### Impact

High
