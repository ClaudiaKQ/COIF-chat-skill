## 2026-06-14 – Separate Job Fit from Business Opportunity Fit

### Context

A WhatsApp job group analysis produced different conclusions before and after using COIF.

The earlier analysis ranked jobs mainly by employment suitability.

The COIF analysis ranked opportunities more broadly, including consulting, partnerships, AI opportunities, and venture creation.

### What Happened

The broader COIF score made some business leads appear above stronger employment opportunities.

This created confusion because Claudia wanted to see both:

1. What jobs are worth applying to
2. What opportunities are useful for CBY Impact

### Lesson

Do not use one combined ranking only.

Always separate:

- Employment Ranking
- Business Development Ranking
- Combined COIF Ranking

### Rule Added

Every WhatsApp analysis must include three rankings and a clean Claudia Next Actions section.

## 2026-06-14 – Claudia Prefers Business-Building Compatible Work

### Context

Claudia clarified that she currently prefers part-time work because she wants to continue building her independent business.

A full-time job is still relevant only if it is exceptional.

### Lesson

The scoring model must prioritize part-time, flexible, consulting, fractional, and partnership opportunities.

### Rule Added

Apply the Employment vs Business Building rule:

Part-time or consulting opportunities may outrank stronger full-time roles if they support CBY Impact.

## 2026-06-14 – Company Name Accuracy Matters

### Context

An opportunity was referred to as “Tel-Hai GIL Center,” but the correct searchable name was closer to:

- GIL Center
- Galilee Innovation Lab
- מרכז GIL ליזמות וחדשנות תל חי

### Lesson

When extracting companies from WhatsApp posts, use the exact searchable name from the source and verify naming before recommending a search phrase.

### Rule Added

Always include:

- Original name from post
- Corrected searchable name
- Hebrew and English search terms when relevant

## 2026-06-14 – OZ Global B2B Case

### Context

OZ Global B2B posted a Digital Team Lead role.

The role itself was not a perfect employment fit because it was marketing/digital execution heavy.

However, the company may be a strong business development lead because it works with B2B tech clients.

### Lesson

A company can be a weak employment fit and a strong consulting or partnership fit.

### Rule Added

For service providers and agencies, always evaluate:

- Client access
- AI/Product strategy partnership potential
- Whether Claudia can become an external specialist partner
