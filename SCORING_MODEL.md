# COIF Scoring Model

This file defines how COIF scores opportunities for Claudia Ben-Yaakov.

COIF evaluates opportunities across multiple value tracks, not only job fit.

---

## Score Scale

Each category is scored from 0 to 10.

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 0–2 | Not relevant |
| 3–4 | Weak fit |
| 5–6 | Possible but not priority |
| 7–8 | Strong opportunity |
| 9–10 | High-priority opportunity |

---

## Core Score Categories

### 1. Employment Fit

How suitable is the role as a job?

Consider:

- Seniority
- Strategic ownership
- Salary potential
- Fit with Claudia's background
- Flexibility
- Hiring probability
- Whether it allows CBY Impact to continue

---

### 2. Consulting Fit

Can Claudia offer value as an independent consultant?

Consider:

- AI product strategy
- AI workflows
- POC design
- Innovation strategy
- PMF
- Product discovery
- Venture creation
- Startup/corporate partnership

---

### 3. Partnership Fit

Can this company become a partner?

Consider:

- Channel potential
- Complementary services
- Shared client base
- Ability to co-create services
- Ecosystem relevance

---

### 4. Client Access Fit

Can this opportunity open access to relevant clients?

Consider:

- Does the company serve startups, tech companies, corporates, investors, or innovation teams?
- Can Claudia reach clients through them?
- Do they have a portfolio, community, or customer base?

---

### 5. AI Opportunity Fit

Is there a clear AI opportunity?

Consider:

- AI workflow
- AI agent
- AI product extension
- AI POC
- AI enablement workshop
- AI productivity system
- AI strategy layer

---

### 6. Venture Creation Fit

Can this lead to a new venture, product, service line, or venture studio activity?

Consider:

- Startup spinout
- Productized consulting
- SaaS idea
- AI-enabled service
- Venture studio collaboration
- Founder matching

---

### 7. Strategic Value Fit

Does this opportunity matter strategically even if it does not produce immediate income?

Consider:

- Brand value
- Network value
- Ecosystem access
- Learning value
- Future client potential
- Reputation leverage

---

### 8. Revenue Potential Fit

Could this produce meaningful income?

Consider:

- Salary
- Consulting fees
- Retainer potential
- Project value
- Referral potential
- Long-term revenue

---

### 9. Accessibility Fit

How easy is it to access the opportunity?

Consider:

- Direct contact available
- Warm connection
- Clear application link
- Contact person is decision maker
- Timing
- Location
- Language
- Relationship path

Accessibility is reported separately and may be used as a tie-breaker.

---

## Default Weighted Final Score

| Category | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Employment Fit | 15% |
| Consulting Fit | 20% |
| Partnership Fit | 15% |
| Client Access Fit | 15% |
| AI Opportunity Fit | 10% |
| Venture Creation Fit | 10% |
| Strategic Value Fit | 10% |
| Revenue Potential Fit | 5% |

Accessibility Fit is usually reported separately.

---

## Personal Preference Multiplier

Before final prioritization, apply Claudia's current strategy:

### Add Priority

+2 priority boost:

- Part-time role
- Fractional role
- Consulting opportunity

+1 priority boost:

- Strong business development potential
- Strong client-access potential
- Strong CBY Impact alignment

### Reduce Priority

-2 penalty:

- Full-time role with low flexibility
- Role focused mainly on execution rather than strategy

-3 penalty:

- Role requiring Claudia to abandon CBY Impact without exceptional compensation or strategic value

### Exception

Remove penalties if the full-time role has:

- Exceptional compensation
- Exceptional strategic value
- Strong AI / Innovation ownership
- Unique seniority
- Strong long-term leverage

## Required Ranking Outputs

Every WhatsApp opportunity analysis must produce three separate rankings:

### Ranking A – Employment

Best jobs to apply for.

Criteria:

- Role fit
- Compensation potential
- Flexibility
- Strategic ownership
- Hiring probability

### Ranking B – Business Development

Best opportunities for:

- Consulting
- Partnerships
- Client acquisition
- AI projects
- Workshops
- Venture creation

### Ranking C – Combined COIF Score

Overall value across all COIF dimensions.

---

## Final Recommendation Labels

Use clear labels:

- Apply
- Contact directly
- Suggest consulting
- Suggest partnership
- Mine client access
- Track only
- Do not invest time

---

## Rule: Employment vs Business Building

Do not assume employment is Claudia's primary objective.

The primary objective is building CBY Impact.

Therefore:

A part-time role, consulting engagement, partnership, or client-access opportunity may rank above a stronger full-time job if it better supports long-term business creation.

However:

Exceptional full-time opportunities with high compensation, strong AI / innovation ownership, or significant strategic value should still be surfaced prominently in the Employment ranking.
